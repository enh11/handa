{On Normal Projective (Injective) Krasner Hypermodules}
{Erg{\"u}l T{\"u}rkmen}
{Department of Mathematics, Faculty of Sciences and Arts, Amasya University, Turkey}     
{}
\begin{abstract}
In this talk, with the help of splitting short exact sequences, characterizations of normal projective and normal injective hypermodules will be provided. In particular, a characterization of hyperrings whose all hypermodules are normally injective (projective) will be given.
\end{abstract}
