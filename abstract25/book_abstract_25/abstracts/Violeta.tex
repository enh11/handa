\begin{abstract_online}
{The Fuzzy Grade of a certain class of Hypergroups}
{Violeta Fotea}
{Al. I. Cuza University, Iasi, Romania}    
{} 
\begin{abstract}
In this presentation, we investigate the fuzzy degree of a hypergroup, which can be associated with genetic heredity. We analyze different cases for the cardinality of the initial hypergroup. This study is a continuation of a previous study.
\end{abstract}
